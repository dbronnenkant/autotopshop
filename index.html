Agostino’s Vinyl Top Service has been in operation since 1973. It is a family owned & operated business run by Vince and Debi Agostino. We are located in Mesa, Arizona on Highway 87, which is also Country Club Drive. Our physical address is 600 S. Country Club Drive, Mesa, AZ 85210, which is on the corner of W. 6th Drive and Country Club. We face Country Club Drive and our sign is on the building.

Agostino’s has a reputation of being honest and offering our customers top-notch service, loyalty, and very fair prices. The Agostino team practices meticulous workmanship that on most occasions goes beyond the call of duty. Vince has always loved the challenge and in many cases has taken on work that other companies have declined.

Vince Agostino has mastered the art of automobiles since 1953. His knowledge has expanded from not only Tops and Upholstery, but to every aspect of a car. His employment with General Motors taught him much working on the assembly line, has attended many technical schools whenever new products hit the market, worked in a body shop from fixing transmissions to painting cars. Vince was a body shop manager for many years in New Jersey prior to settling in Arizona in 1973. He has endless experience dealing with insurance companies and their adjustors, and has shared his knowledge with family members working by his side. Vince has a reputation here in the valley that is admired by many of his colleagues.
